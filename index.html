About me

Having spent about 20 years in retail at all levels from entry level cashier to store manager, I decided it was a time for a change. I bring some unique skillsets, including project management, hard work ethic, and a desire to learn and build upon my knowledge base.


It is my hope to take what I learn in this bootcamp and apply it to my personal and professional goal of creating something that will bring someone joy. In other words, I hope to design and build my own game.
